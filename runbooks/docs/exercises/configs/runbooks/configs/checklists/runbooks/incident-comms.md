# Incident Communication Templates

Clear communication reduces panic and restores trust. Use these templates during outages or attacks.

---

## 1) Internal update (Slack/Teams)

**Title:** Incident Update — [Service] — [Severity]  
**Time:** [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM]  
**Status:** Investigating / Identified / Mitigating / Monitoring / Resolved

**What we see**
- Symptoms:
- Impact:
- Affected users/regions:

**What we’re doing**
- Actions taken:
- Next action:
- Owner(s):

**Next update**
- Time of next update:
- Where updates will be posted:

---

## 2) Public status page update

**Incident:** [Short title]  
**Started:** [Time]  
**Impact:** [What users experience]

**Update**
We are currently [investigating/mitigating] an issue affecting [service].  
Users may experience [symptoms].  
Next update in [X minutes].

---

## 3) Customer support response (email/chat)

Hello,  
We’re aware of an issue affecting [service] and are actively working to restore full availability.  
Current impact: [symptoms].  
Workaround (if any): [steps].  
We will share updates via [status page/link].  
Thank you for your patience.

---

## 4) Post-incident public summary (after resolution)

**What happened**
- (Brief factual summary)

**Impact**
- Who was affected and how

**What we did**
- Immediate mitigation steps

**Prevention**
- What changes we’re making to reduce recurrence

**Support**
- How affected users can get help
